Who? What?

I am a Christian, husband, and father. I have adopted my church's mission statement for my life: "I exist to spread a passion for the supremacy of God in all things for the joy of all peoples through Jesus Christ." I live this mission poorly, screwing up all along the way, yet trusting in the Jesus of the Christian scriptures for life and breath and everything.

I was a civil engineer, I thought I would pastor, now I administrate at Bethlehem College & Seminary in Minneapolis, MN. I have a mediocre blog that includes thoughts, quotes, and other stuff probably only I care about. I have written some stuff, but not enough that's very good. I have also preached a few times.

If you are interested, check out my blog, writing, and speaking.
